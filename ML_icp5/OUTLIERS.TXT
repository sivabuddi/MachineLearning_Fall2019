2. Procedure to identify outliers

a) Arrange the data in increasing order.
b) Calculate first(q1) and third quartile(q3)
c) Find interquartile range (q3-q1)
d) Find lower bound q1*1.5.
e) Find upper bound q3*1.5.
f) Anything that lies outside of lower and upper bound is an outlier.
